What We Build

Our Core Services

From elegant main gates to precision-crafted railings — every piece engineered for lasting strength and refined aesthetics.

01
Main Gates

Custom-designed compound and driveway gates in iron and SS. Sliding, swing, and automatic options for homes and commercial properties.

02
SS Railings

Staircase and balcony railings in 304-grade stainless steel. Mirror polish or matte finish. Architecturally precise and maintenance-free.

03
Iron Chaukhat

Heavy-duty door frames in Apollo Iron, precisely welded for perfect fit. Corrosion-resistant finish for decades of dependable use.

04
Window Grills

Decorative and security grills for residential and commercial windows. Custom patterns, powder-coated in your choice of colour.

Our Methodology

How We Work

A transparent, four-step process that ensures quality at every stage — from your doorstep to final installation.

01
Site Visit & Measurement

Our expert visits your property to take precise measurements, assess the site, and understand your exact requirements.

02
Design Selection

Choose from our portfolio or share your inspiration. We draft a custom design with detailed 2D layout for your approval.

03
Precision Fabrication

Using Apollo Iron and 304 SS, our skilled welders fabricate your piece with CNC-level precision in our workshop.

04
Professional Installation

Our installation team fits everything perfectly on-site with full cleanup. Quality checked and handed over with a service warranty.
